Core Features

Preplok combines AI intelligence with thousands of real exam questions to give Nigerian students the most effective prep tool available.

AI-Powered Hints

Stuck on a question? Our AI engine generates smart, contextual hints that guide your thinking without giving away the answer — so you actually learn.

Vast Past Questions Bank

Access thousands of authentic past questions from WAEC, JAMB, NECO, Post UTME, and GCE — curated, verified, and organized by subject and year.

Timed Mock Exams

Simulate the real exam experience with timed sessions that mirror actual exam conditions — complete with scoring, question randomization, and pressure-testing.

Performance Analytics

Detailed dashboards show your strengths, weak spots by topic, score trends over time, and how you compare against your personal bests — personalized for you.

Detailed AI Explanations

Every answer comes with a full AI-generated explanation — breaking down the reasoning, relevant concepts, and common mistakes so you truly understand, not just memorize.

Personalized Study Plans

Preplok adapts to your pace. Based on your performance data, it recommends topics to focus on, sets daily study targets, and helps you build consistent exam-ready habits.

Mobile-First Design

Designed for Nigerian students on the go — study on your Android or iOS device anywhere, even with limited connectivity, with a clean and intuitive interface.

Adaptive Difficulty

Questions get smarter as you do. Our adaptive engine progressively challenges you based on your performance, ensuring you're always growing and never bored.

Freemium Access

Start for free — no credit card needed. Access mock exams and past questions at no cost, then upgrade for advanced AI features, unlimited practice, and deeper insights.

Future Roadmap

Where Preplok is headed

We are committed to continuous innovation — building Preplok into a comprehensive educational ecosystem for every Nigerian learner.

Short-Term (1–2 Years)

Launch live tutoring via video calls, community forums for peer discussion, subject-specific video lectures, and voice-assisted AI hints for accessibility — including support for visually impaired learners.

Medium-Term (2–3 Years)

Expand to university past questions from UNILAG, UI, ABU and more. Introduce AI-driven adaptive learning paths tailored to each user's weaknesses, covering Engineering, Medicine, Law, and all major courses.

Long-Term (3+ Years)

Expand to other African exams (e.g., KCSE in Kenya), integrate VR/AR for immersive science simulations, partner with global edtech firms for certifications, and offer AI-generated custom questions with predictive analytics.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, engaging, and effective exam preparation tools that leverage past questions, mock simulations, and AI insights — helping students master subjects, build confidence, and achieve academic excellence.

Our Vision

To become the leading AI-powered educational platform in Nigeria, transforming how students prepare for exams and pursue higher education — making high-quality, personalized learning accessible to everyone, regardless of location or resources.

"We envision a future where Preplok eliminates barriers to education, reduces failure rates in national exams, and equips a generation of confident, knowledgeable individuals ready to contribute to Nigeria's growth."
