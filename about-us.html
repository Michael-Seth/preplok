Transforming Education in Nigeria with AI

Preplok is an innovative educational platform designed to empower Nigerian students by making high-quality, personalized learning accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

To become the leading AI-powered educational platform in Nigeria, transforming how students prepare for exams and pursue higher education. We envision a future where Preplok eliminates barriers to education, reduces failure rates in national exams, and equips a generation of confident, knowledgeable individuals ready to contribute to Nigeria's growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide affordable, engaging, and effective exam preparation tools that leverage past questions, mock simulations, and AI insights to help students master subjects, build confidence, and achieve academic excellence. We are committed to addressing local educational challenges and promoting inclusive learning.

What We Offer

Preplok serves as a comprehensive platform for learning and exam preparation, focusing on key national examinations.

National Exams

Comprehensive prep for WAEC, JAMB, NECO, GCE, and Post UTME exams.

AI Guidance

Integration of AI-driven hints and detailed explanations for every question.

Performance Analytics

Realistic mock environments with timed sessions, scoring, and analytics.

Our Goals

We are committed to measurable progress and long-term impact.

User Engagement

Attract 100,000 active users by offering free basic access to mock exams and past questions.

Content Excellence

Curate an accurate, up-to-date database of past questions covering all major subjects.

AI Enhancement

Continuous improvement of AI models to provide 95% accurate hints and explanations.
